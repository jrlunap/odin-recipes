Carrot Muffins

Description

This recipe for Gluten-Free Carrot Oat Muffins yields 12 moist, naturally sweetened muffins using oat flour and your choice of walnuts or pecans.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oat flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cup sugar (or maple syrup)
  • 1/3 cup oil
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk (or milk of choice)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of freshly grated carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans

Steps

  1. Prep the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a standard 12-cup muffin pan with paper liners.
  2. Mix Wet Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together the oil and sugar. Add the eggs one at a time, whisking until frothy, then stir in the milk and vanilla.
  3. Incorporate Dry Ingredients: Pour the oat flour over the wet mixture. Sprinkle the baking powder, baking soda, salt, and spices on top. Stir gently until just combined—be careful not to overmix, as this can make oat flour muffins tough.
  4. Add Textures: Fold in the grated carrots and most of the nuts (reserve a few for the tops if desired).
  5. Bake: Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups. Bake for 15–20 minutes. The tops should be golden and spring back when pressed gently.
  6. Cool: Let the muffins sit in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
